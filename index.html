About Me

I'm a passionate explorer at the intersection of technology, consciousness, and beauty. My work and interests span across various domains, most recently focusing on driving the adoption of AI, filling the gap between current AI capabilities and realising its value, in a safe and trustworthy manner. My life purpose is in seeking to understand and create meaningful bridge between technology and the human experience.

I love to read science and philosophy, meditate, hike and am curious about the world around me. I also enjoying doing my part to stay fit with workout. Motivated by self-improvement and reaching greater inner clarity, I am open to seeking out adventures, try my hand at many things, face challenges both within and in the world. Most recently I enjoyed trying kite surfing!

My Journey

I was born in Hong Kong

At just a few months of age, my parents moved to live in Nepal with my sister. I grew up loving art and drawing, and also developed interests in science, maths and taekwondo through my parents encouragement.

At the age of 7, I moved to Tanzania mostly in a boarding school until the age of 18. I had some of my greatest experiences growing up in the international community of ISM, including climbing Mt. Kilimanjaro and making friends and connections that remain to this day.

I moved to the UK at the age of 18 for university, studying Law and Philosophy at first (and the only student to do so) at University of Kent. I also made friendships here which I maintain to this day!

My interests branched from here, which lead me to study two masters, the first in Language and Cognition at Kings College London and the second in Cognitive Science at UCL.

After working part-time while completing my masters, I decided to move into industry and start a career as a Data Scientist. I had the privilege of working across many interesting projects and organisations.

With 6+ years of experience now, I thoroughly enjoy building solutions for clients and have enjoyed and shared in the recent excitement of building AI applications and finding solutions to deploy these at production for clients. I look forward to improving my capacity to deliver meaningful work, develop my skills and understanding, and increase the footprint of my impact into this world in the way I was meant to.
