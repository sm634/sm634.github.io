About Me

My work and interests span across various domains, most recently focusing on driving the adoption of AI to fill the gap between current AI capabilities and realising its value, in a safe and trustworthy manner. While there are many worries about AI, I believe the correct and ethical implementation of AI will expand current boundaries for human exploration and consciousness.

My life purpose is in seeking to understand and create a meaningful bridge between technology and the human experience.

I love to read science and philosophy, meditate, hike and am curious about the world around me. I also enjoy doing my part to stay fit and working out, while remaining a foodie. Motivated by self-improvement and reaching greater inner clarity, I am open to seeking out adventures, try my hand at many things, face challenges both within and in the world. Most recently I enjoyed trying kite surfing!

My Journey so Far

I was born in Hong Kong.

At just a few months of age, my parents moved to live in Nepal with my sister. I grew up loving art and drawing, and also developed interests in science, maths and taekwondo through my parents encouragement.

At the age of 7, I moved to Tanzania mostly staying at a boarding school until the age of 18. I had some of my greatest experiences growing up in the international community of ISM, including climbing Mt. Kilimanjaro and making friends and connections that remain to this day.

I moved to the UK at the age of 18 for university, studying Law and Philosophy (and the only student in the joint course) at University of Kent. I also made friendships here which I maintain to this day!

My interests branched out from here, which led me to study two masters, the first in Language and Cognition (again the only student) at Kings College London and the second in Cognitive Science at UCL.

After working part-time while completing my masters, I decided to move into industry and start a career as a Data Scientist. I had the privilege of working across many interesting projects and organisations, spanning from a first of a kind native cloud data engineering and MLOps project for a public body, to building AI Assistants. I picked up many skills such as programming with python, data science, enterprise architecture, using cloud technology and many more. I also enjoyed working with my clients ranging from HR admins to CTOs! Aided by my appetite for learning, I found opportunities and moved across various roles from a lead Data Engineer, Data Scientist, AI Engineer, to non-delivery focused roles as an AI Specialty Architect, Technical Account Manager and Solution Architect for both pre and post-sales.

With 6+ years of experience now, I thoroughly enjoy the end-to-end challenges of working with clients and teams in designing solutions and have shared in the recent excitement of building AI applications and deploying to production for clients. I look forward to improving my capacity to deliver meaningful work, develop my skills and understanding of technology, business and the world, and increase the footprint of my impact the way I was meant to.
